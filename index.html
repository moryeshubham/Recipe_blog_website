Maggi With Chicken Recipe

Preparation Time : 10 mins

Cooking Time :15 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 2packet maggi
    1. maggi masala
  • chopped onion
  • chicken slices
  • oil
  • salt
  • water

How To Cook :

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add maagi packet in hot water and stir well.
  3. Add maggi masala and pinch of salt.
  4. Add onion to hot oil.
  5. After a minute , add chicken slices in onion and hot oil mixture.
  6. Add salt and masala.
  7. Mix all and give it all a good stir.
